What are you known for?


I am known for the games Lords of Waterdeep, Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, and Star Wars Saga Edition.


What are some of your favorite games?


The Manhattan Project, Hansa Teutonica, Agricola, Shogun, and TowerFall.


What do you look for in a game?


Games where the decisions I make matter, games where the mechanics are simple to understand but lead to complex decision-making, games that let you make choices that make you feel clever, games that give you both short-term and long-term goals and rewards.
