Mike Selinker

Find him at @mikeselinker.


What are you known for?


I'm a designer or developer of games like the Pathfinder Adventure Card Game, Unspeakable Words, and Betrayal at House on the Hill. I also write puzzles for the New York Times, Wired magazine, and Cards Against Humanity, among many others. I’ve written a few books: Puzzlecraft, Dealer’s Choice, The Kobold Guide to Board Game Design, and the new puzzle novel The Maze of Games. I own a little game studio called Lone Shark Games, and we make cool things.


What are some of your favorite games?


I've been known to play a lot of D&D, Bohnanza, Set, and poker. And kinda way too much Ascension on the iPad.


What do you look for in a game?


I have almost no long term memory, so I need something I haven’t seen before. If I can't think of a brilliant thing about a game, I probably won't remember it exists.
