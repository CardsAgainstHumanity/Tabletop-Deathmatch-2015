Annalisa Delfel

Find her at @chaosadora.


What are you known for?


I was the retail manager of the Seattle game store Card Kingdom for a decade, and helped see it through several incarnations, including the wonderful store it is today.


What are some of your favorite games?


I tend to be a traditionalist, but some of my favorites are Last Night On Earth, Pandemic, and Battlestar Galactica, as well as an unending love for Boggle.


What do you look for in a game?


I look for games that I can play with people who aren't necessarily gamers, which means they need to be easy to navigate, but have enough substance to hold interest. For me, the biggest obstacle to enjoying a game would be overly complicated rules and actions. Sometimes simple is best.
