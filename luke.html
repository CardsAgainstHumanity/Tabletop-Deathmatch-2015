Luke Crane

What are you known for?


I am the designer of the Burning Wheel, Mouse Guard, and Torchbearer.
I also work as the Community Manager for Games at Kickstarter.


What are some of your favorite games?


D&D, Burning Wheel (duh), Command & Colors: Napoleonics, and Mario Kart.


What do you look for in a game?


In games, I want to see theme married closely to system, so that when the player makes a choice in the game, it exists within the context of both the world of the game and the strategy of the moment.
