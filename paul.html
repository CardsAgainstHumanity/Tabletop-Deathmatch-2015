What are you known for?


I'm a designer and developer of many kinds of games from board and card to mobile and web, including Smash Up, Guillotine, Bejeweled Blitz, and Bella Sara.


What are some of your favorite games?


My favorites change a lot, but currently they include Tokaido, Lords of Waterdeep, and Caverna, as well as an ever-changing rotation of mobile games.


What do you look for in a game?


I look for an elegant set of rules, mechanics that work well with each other, innovation, and a strong theme. Overall, though, I look for a game with fun interactions with my friends.
